STARBUCKS

Starbucks is a global coffee company and coffeehouse chain that was founded in 1971 in Seattle, Washington. It has grown to become one of the most recognizable and influential coffee brands worldwide.

Abo​ut us

Starbucks was born out of a passion for the rich tradition of coffee and a desire to create a unique place where people could gather, connect, and share their love for a great cup of coffee. Founded in 1971 in Seattle's historic Pike Place Market, Starbucks began as a single store, offering freshly roasted coffee beans, teas, and spices from around the world. Since then, we've grown to become the world's leading coffeehouse brand, with more than 33,000 stores in over 80 countries.


Whether you're grabbing your morning coffee on the go, meeting up with friends, or looking for a quiet place to work, Starbucks is here for you. We’re proud to be part of your daily routine and are committed to making your Starbucks experience special every time you visit.
